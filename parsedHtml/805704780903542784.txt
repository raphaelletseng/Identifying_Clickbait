What It's Like to Live on Mars
Government agencies and private companies are competing in a 21st-century space race to get the first human beings to the Red Planet. With plans for spacecrafts outfitted with all the trappings of a cruise ship and fully fledged, self-sustaining colonies on the planet, it’s not “if” we’ll go—but “when.” And on whose rocket ship.
