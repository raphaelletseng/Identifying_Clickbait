Watch the Red Carpet Live Stream at Billboard Women in Music 2016

It's time for the 10th annual Billboard Women in Music.

The 2016 red carpet show -- hosting by Billboard's Keith Caulfield and Chelsea Briggs -- will air live from Billboard's Facebook, beginning at 11:40AM ET.

At the event, Madonna will receive the Woman of the Year Award. Shania Twain is this year's Icon. Halsey is the Rising Star, Alessia Cara the Rule Breaker, Andra Day the Powerhouse, Maren Morris the Breakthrough, Meghan Trainor the Chart Topper, and Kesha the Trailblazer.

Academy Award nominee Hailee Steinfeld is hosting the ceremony.
