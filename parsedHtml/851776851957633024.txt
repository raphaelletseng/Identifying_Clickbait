Best places to see flowers in Japan
19 photographs that prove Japan is the most beautiful place on Earth right now                     Japan's spectacular cherry blossom season is in full swing. "It is impossible to think of springtime Japan without an iconic image of a sea of cherry trees awash with perfect pink blooms instantly coming to mind," says Danielle Demetriou, our expert to the country. "When the blooms actually arrive (as confirmed by teams of meticulous cherry blossom officials), it is time to indulge in one of the nation’s all-time favourite pastimes – hanami, which refers to flower appreciation picnics under the blooms. Every year, a microcosm of society – from salarymen and students to housewives and grannies – takes part in hanami picnics (some civilised, some rowdy) in every corner of the country."
