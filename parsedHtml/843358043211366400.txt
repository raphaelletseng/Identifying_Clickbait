Police make arrest after man shot in head on Brisbane's southside

Posted

Police have arrested a man wanted over a shooting on Brisbane's southside last night.

The 32-year-old man was taken into custody just after 2:00pm Queensland time at Hawthorne in Brisbane's east.

A 38-year-old man remains in hospital after he was shot in the abdomen and head at Holland Park West just before 10pm on Saturday.

Police say the men knew each other.
