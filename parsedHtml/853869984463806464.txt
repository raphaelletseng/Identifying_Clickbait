Bruce Highway crash: Fatal four-vehicle collision closes major road near Maryborough

Posted

At least one person has died after a four-vehicle crash near Maryborough on Queensland's Bruce Highway, police say.

Emergency crews attended the scene just after 3:00pm.

Police have closed the highway in both directions, and have confirmed one fatality.

Ambulance crews are treating eight patients at the scene.

Two helicopters are on scene, according to Queensland Ambulance.
