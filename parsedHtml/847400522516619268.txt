Australian man dies after jumping from Bangkok airport's fourth floor

Posted

Thai police say an Australian citizen has died after jumping from the fourth floor of Bangkok's international airport.

The 32-year-old man, who held both held Australian and Irish passports, was sent to hospital but died about two hours later.

Police said a review of the Suvarnabhumi Airport's security footage showed the incident took place in the early hours of the morning.

The ABC is withholding the man's name until confirming his next of kin have been notified.
