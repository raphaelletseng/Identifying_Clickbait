7 Times Shannen Doherty Inspired Us to Never Give Up


Happy birthday to Shannen Doherty! Born in Memphis, Tenn., Doherty is known for her roles as Brenda Walsh in Beverly Hills, 90210, Prue Halliwell in Charmed, and Heather Duke in Heathers. The actress was diagnosed with Crohn's disease in 1999, and nearly 15 years later, she was diagnosed with breast cancer. In 2016, the cancer spread to her lymphatic system, and her days were filled with chemotherapy and radiotherapy. As of today, Doherty has completed cancer treatment and her strength continues to inspire us all.
