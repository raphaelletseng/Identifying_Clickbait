This Restaurant's Comfort Food Will Make You Want To Be A Kid Again
Queens Comfort in Queens, New York makes the kind of comfort food you wish your mom had made you as a kid. ^ That would be a funfetti cake donut with Nutella glaze and frosted animal crackers Many of the items on the menu are childhood-inspired: Cheesy tater tots > normal tater tots. Or sprinkled and/or covered in cereal: Even babies love it. But most of the stuff on the menu is just comfort food kicked up, like, a thousand notches. Like these guacamole-stuffed onion rings: Or the mac ‘n’ cheese parm, in which fried chicken tender slices are tucked in a MAC ‘N’ CHEESE DIP. …Even when the exterior cheese shell cools, you can pull a fried chicken strip to reveal the still-gooey cheese within. And to set the tone, the walls and tables are crammed with nostalgic throwbacks: If you’re in town and decide to make the trek, just remember the restaurant commands lines even in the dead of winter.
