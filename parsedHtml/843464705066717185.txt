Suspect charged with attempted murder after man shot in head on Brisbane's southside

Updated

Police have charged a man wanted over a shooting on Brisbane's southside on Saturday night.

A 32-year-old man, who is known to the victim, was arrested at Hawthorne in Brisbane's east on Sunday afternoon.

A 38-year-old man remains in hospital after he was shot in the abdomen and head at Holland Park West just before 10:00pm on Saturday.

The suspect has been charged with attempted murder, acts intended to cause grievous bodily harm, wilful damage, unlawful possession of a weapon, unlawful discharge of a weapon and possession of a dangerous drug.

He is scheduled to appear in Brisbane Magistrates Court on Monday.
