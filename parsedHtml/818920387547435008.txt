Kim Kardashian robbery may have been ‘inside job,’ investigators say

New details are emerging about the armed robbery of an estimated $10 million worth of jewelry from Kim Kardashian West last year. Officials have confirmed that her chauffeur is among a slew of new arrests in the case, pointing to the possibility of an inside job. NBC’s Keir Simmons reports for TODAY.
