Sales and Rentals of Movies for Home Viewing Continue Slide 
Biography Ben Fritz @benfritz ben.fritz@wsj.com Hollywood’s home entertainment business continued to worsen in 2016, as DVD rentals plummeted and growth in digital purchases slowed. Total revenue from people watching movies at home, which contributes more to studios’ bottom line than the box office, was $12.05 billion in the U.S., down 7% from 2015. In 2015, the total dropped 6% to $12.95 billion, according to the Digital Entertainment Group, an industry trade organization.
