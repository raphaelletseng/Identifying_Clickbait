In 2011, the festival went on for 10 days wherein more than 15,000 dogs were slaughtered and eaten. This is only an estimated figure and the numbers can be higher.

Reuters

The unfortunate dogs are imprisoned at this festival are routinely “snared around the neck with metal hooks and dragged from their cages.

They are forced to travel a long distance, crammed into wired cages, and gets eaten by their fate. Many are burned and boiled alive.

Reuters

Not only strays but even pet dogs with their collars still on are stolen from all over the country. Close to 226,800 kilogrammes of dog meat is consumed every year.

Reuters

The city of Yulin has over 100 dog slaughter houses that butcher as many as 2000 dogs a day during the festival.

So when this disturbing act caught world's attention, a lot of animal lovers and activists shouted in unison for its abandonment, however, nothing concrete was fetched out of it. Despite several petitions, China will host this event anyway this year in June.

Reuters
