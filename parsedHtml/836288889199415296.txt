Reykjavik time-lapse captures overnight snowfall
Reykjavik time-lapse captures overnight snowfall - BBC News


Iceland's capital has seen its biggest snowfall in 80 years, with 51cm (20in) falling over the weekend.

Reykjavik resident Jón Atli Magnússon set his camera to time-lapse when he went to bed on Saturday night. This was the result on Sunday morning.
