Watch Ariana Grande and Mac Miller Serenade Each Other in 'My Favorite Part' Video

If Ariana Grande and Mac Miller wanted to play-down their rumored romance, the video for “My Favorite Part” blows all that out the water.

The new clip features Grande as the girl next door, and Miller as the patient love interest. Song is in the air, rain is tumbling down outside, a romance seems inevitable... but a wall is ruining their evening.

"You just don't know how beautiful you are/ And baby that's my favorite part/ You walk around so clueless to it all/ Like nobody gonna break your heart," croons Miller. The pair serenade each other with sweet words, and Cupid hits his target.

The duet appears on Miller’s fourth studio album The Divine Feminine, which dropped in September and peaked at No. 2 on the Billboard 200.
