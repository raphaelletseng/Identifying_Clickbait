After releasing a string of records, including "CRZY," "Distraction" and "Advice" from her forthcoming debut album, SweetSexySavage, Kehlani is now preparing to embark on a 60-date world tour.

The SweetSexySavage trek will find the Oakland songstress in Montreal, Canada, on Feb. 21. She'll then touch down in New York and Boston before taking her set overseas and making stops in cities including Paris, Zurich and Vienna. Once she makes her rounds in Europe, she'll venture back to the U.S. and wrap her tour in Los Angeles on June 18.

Joining Kehlani on her world tour will be U.K. sensation Ella Mai and Toronto's very own Jahkoy, who will serve as her openers. Pre-sales will begin Dec. 13 on her official website while general sales and VIP packages will go wide on Dec. 14.

Kehlani's debut album SweetSexySavage is set for release on Jan. 27.
