Afghan 'green-eyed girl' Sharbat Gula in quest for a new life
Afghan 'green-eyed girl' Sharbat Gula in quest for a new life - BBC News


An Afghan woman made famous by a 1985 National Geographic cover talks exclusively to the BBC of her hopes for a new beginning. Sharbat Gula spoke to BBC Afghan after being deported to Afghanistan from Pakistan where she lived as a refugee for 35 years.
