Former President George W. Bush, former First Lady Laura Bush, former President Bill Clinton and former First Lady Hillary Clinton will be attending President-elect Donald Trump's inauguration.

Despite being a fellow Republican, Bush did not vote for Trump on Election Day.

Former President Bill Clinton and 2016 Democratic presidential nominee Hillary Clinton will also be in attendance at the inauguration, aides to both Clintons told CNN on Tuesday.
