Sam Moore Sings 'America the Beautiful' at Trump Inaugural Concert

Sam Moore of legendary soul duo Sam & Dave performed "America the Beautiful" at the Thursday (Jan. 19) inaugural concert for Donald Trump.

Introduced by Jon Voight as a "Gramming award winning singer," Moore and a backup choir belted the patriotic anthem.

Moore sang after a performance from Mohawk-sporting drummer RaviDrums, but before a group of military men and women dressed up as American Revolution soldiers played fifes and drums.

On Friday, Jan. 20, Trump will be sworn in as the 45th president of the United States.
