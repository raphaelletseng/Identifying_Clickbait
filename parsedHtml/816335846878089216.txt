The lineup for the 2017 BottleRock Napa Valley music festival was unveiled Tuesday morning (Jan. 3), revealing Foo Fighters, Tom Petty & The Heartbreakers and Maroon 5 as the weekend's headliners.

The three-day music, wine, food, and craft brew festival, which is held over Memorial Day weekend (May 26 through May 28), will take place at the Napa Valley Expo in the heart of wine country.

Along with the headliners, more than 80 other artists will perform over the BottleRock weekend, including Macklemore & Ryan Lewis, Modest Mouse, Ben Harper & The Innocent Criminals, The Roots, Silversun Pickups, Band of Horses, Fitz & The Tantrums, Dirty Heads, Live, The Naked and Famous, Michael Franti & Spearhead, Gavin DeGraw, St. Lucia, House of Pain, Bob Moses, Catfish and the Bottlemen, Saint Motel, The Strumbellas, AlunaGeorge and Andra Day.

Passes for the festival will go on sale at 10 a.m. PST on Jan. 4 at BottleRockNapaValley.com and eventbrite.com. See the full lineup here.
