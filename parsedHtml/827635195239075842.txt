Ukraine: Inside civilians' living nightmare
Ukraine: Inside civilians' living nightmare - BBC News


In eastern Ukraine, one woman has told the BBC she cannot tell her grandson his mother is dead after another night of heavy shelling.

Government forces and Russian-backed rebels have accused each other of attacking civilians as fighting intensifies, with some of the heaviest clashes just over 10 miles from rebel-held Donetsk.

Tom Burridge reports from the city of Avdiivka.
