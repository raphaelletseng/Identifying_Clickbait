Boys Noize had one hell of a year. He released his fourth proper LP Mayday, crafted a new live show, then toured the world blowing minds with his brooding, apocalyptic street beats. There were a lot of moments worth capturing, so he took a videographer with him on tour and set the whole year to dope remixes of his Mayday tracks.

Feast your eyes on a serious dose of international FOMO and experience 2016 as Boys Noize's blur. The three-minute clip features three tracks from the upcoming Mayday Remixxx Album, a live intro of “Overthrow,” Boys Noize and MXM's take on “Euphoria,” and an alternate version of “Starchild.” Footage includes shots of Berlin, Barcelona, Brooklyn, Los Angeles, and more.
