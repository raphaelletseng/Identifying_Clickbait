Congress Scion Rahul Gandhi and UP CM Akhilesh Yadav today held a press conference in Lucknow after the alliance between SP and Congress was forged at the behest of Priyanka Gandhi.

The Gandhi-Yadav duo answered numerous question hurled at them by journalist and while answering some of the serious questions, the duo didn't leave any chance to take a jibe at rivals including Prime Minister Narendra Modi and BSP Supremo, Mayawati.

Akhilesh especially took jibe at PM Modi and said, "Mann Chanchal hota hain. Jyada Mann Ki Baat Mat Suniye, Nahi To Fas Jaenge."

Rahul Gandhi almost one-upped him by adding this valuable insight: history static cheez nahi hoti hei, history badalti rehti hei!
