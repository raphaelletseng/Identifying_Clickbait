Frank Mason III Wins 2016-17 Naismith Trophy
Kansas guard Frank Mason III has won the 2016-17 Naismith Trophy, an award given to the best college basketball player in the country, per the team's official Twitter account . Mason led the Big 12 in scoring at 20.9 points per game to go with 5.2 assists and 4.2 rebounds per contest. The senior led the Jayhawks to their 13th consecutive regular-season title in the Big 12 as well as a No. 1 seed in the NCAA tournament. They reached the Elite Eight before falling to Oregon.
