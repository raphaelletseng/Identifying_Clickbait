Ryan Reynolds Is Getting A Star On The Hollywood Walk Of Fame
Ryan Reynolds is cementing his status in Hollywood history. On Dec. 15, the “Deadpool” headliner will be honored with a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame. Anna Faris, who co-starred with Reynolds in 2005’s “Just Friends” and “Waiting…,” will act as a guest speaker. Rhett Reese and Paul Wernick, who wrote the screenplays for “Deadpool” and Reynold’s upcoming film, “Life,” will also speak. The ceremony will occur at 11:30 a.m. PST (2:30 p.m. EST) on Thursday.
