Return to East Timor: 'I've been brought back from the dead'
Return to East Timor: 'I've been brought back from the dead' - BBC News


Alberto Mohamed is one of about 4,000 children taken by the Indonesian authorities in the 1980s and 1990s, during Indonesia's brutal occupation of East Timor.

Now a local NGO is trying to reunite them with their families.
