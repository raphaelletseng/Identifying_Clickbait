Well, here we are -- 2016 is nearly over, and as we look back on a miserable year, we're left with quite a bit of incredible music as a small consolation prize.

From masterpieces like Beyonce's Lemonade and David Bowie's Blackstar to thrilling debuts from Gallant, Maren Morris and King to creative leaps into the future from The 1975 and Chance the Rapper, the Must-Hear Music podcast crew (Joe Lynch, Jason Lipshutz and Natalie Weiner) discuss the year's most acclaimed albums as well as our personal favorites. Jason and Natalie argue about the value of Anderson .Paak's Malibu, Jason and Joe go back-and-forth on Anohni's Hopelessness, and we all give our picks for 2016 releases that were incredible but too long, or impressive but a chore to listen to.
