Mariah Carey Drops New YG Collab 'I Don't': Listen

Mariah Carey has dropped “I Don’t,” her new collaboration with YG.

The pop superstar briefly discussed the track during a recent Facebook Live session, noting that she and YG worked on it with Jermaine Dupri. The song was also teased on the Jan. 29 season finale of Mariah’s World.

"But I'm tired of cryin, no more tears/Pity party of the year/Cold at night cause you're not here/Leaving you was my worst fear," Carey sings.

Carey said during her Facebook Live stream that she’s keen to do more single releases like "I Don't", rather than release a full album. Her 14th and most recent LP was Me. I Am Mariah...the Elusive Chanteuse, which dropped in 2014 and peaked at No. 3 on the Billboard 200.
