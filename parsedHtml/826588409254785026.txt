Kanye West’s Yeezy Season 5 is set to show during New York Fashion Week, on Feb. 15, according to Fashionista.

The multi-hyphenate will reportedly unveil his latest line in partnership with Adidas at Pier 59 Studios. And unlike Yeezy’s previously used locations (Madison Square Garden, Roosevelt Island), it will be much easier to access, at NYFW’s frequented Chelsea Pier location.

Yeezy Season 5 will be showing after Anna Sui -- a favorite of Madonna and Jack White -- and before New York designer Thom Browne’s 6 p.m. slot, Fashionista reports. The meticulous programming of Season 5 strays from West’s previous pop-up style runway shows, but will hopefully contain as many surprises.

Billboard has reached out to Kanye's team for further comment.
