South African astrologer fights to legalise 'magic' mushrooms
South African astrologer fights to legalise 'magic' mushrooms - BBC News


Psiciliscybin mushrooms, also known as “magic” mushrooms, are banned in many countries around the world due to their hallucinogenic qualities.

But one 72-year-old-woman in South Africa is fighting through the courts to legalise their use for spiritual ceremonies.

Monica Cromhout faces a possible jail sentence for dealing in illegal substances.
