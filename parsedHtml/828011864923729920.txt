A Wave of New Works by Gerhard Richter
German artist  Gerhard Richter turns 85 next Thursday, and a museum in his home city Cologne will use the opportunity to mount an eye-catching birthday celebration: an exhibition bursting with his new paintings. What’s unusual is that Mr. Richter will unveil 26 abstract works all completed in 2016 in a museum rather than in a commercial gallery.
