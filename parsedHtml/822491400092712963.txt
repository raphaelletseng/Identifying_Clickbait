Big Sean and Aziz Ansari in a promo for Saturday Night Live.

Big Sean will be making moves with comedian Aziz Ansari for his Saturday Night Live debut on Saturday (Jan. 21).

In a new promo with the pair, SNL star Kate McKinnon jokingly calls herself "Medium Kate." Sean then retorts, "That's not how it works" before Ansari, who will serve as host, loudly repeats it for emphasis.

The comedian also shoots his own version of Sean's "Moves" video in a separate teaser as he strolls through Studio 8H before picking up a call from his mother.

Big Sean's SNL show precedes the arrival of his fourth studio effort I Decided dropping Feb. 3.
