Real-life horror at Kong: Skull Island Vietnam premiere
Real-life horror at Kong: Skull Island Vietnam premiere - BBC News


Crowds were shocked at the sizzling premiere of Kong: Skull Island in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.

A five-metre high King Kong model was set alight, after performers danced around a fire on a stage.

Much of the new film was shot in the Vietnamese capital Hanoi and other areas of the country.
