North Korea test-fires ballistic missile, South Korean media say

Posted

North Korea has test-fired a ballistic missile from a region north of Pyongyang, Yonhap news agency reported, citing South Korea's military.

There were no immediate details about the missile or its flight, Yonhap said.

Initial indications were that the missile test was unsuccessful, according to a US Government source.
