"It's a whole project. Also a lifestyle."

As Post Malone rides the wave of success from his debut album, Stoney -- released earlier this month -- the Texas singer, songwriter and rapper has already announced its follow-up.

Beerbongs & Bentleys is "a whole project. also a lifestyle and it’s coming,” Malone tweeted Thursday.

beerbongs and bentleys isn't a song.... It's a whole project. also a lifestyle --and it's coming -- — Beerbongs & Bentleys (@PostMalone) December 29, 2016

Malone also changed his Twitter name to "Beerbongs & Bentleys."

The news comes after a post last week in which he shared video of himself in the studio rocking out to a new track.

After several delays, Stoney arrived in early December and debuted at No. 6 on the Billboard 200. Malone first stepped into the spotlight in August 2015 with his breakout single, "White Iverson."
