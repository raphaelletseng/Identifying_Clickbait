"If there was a dormant account or one where the volume was very low and suddenly it saw a spurt in the form of large deposits, we decided to play it safe and report it to FIU. We are not taking any chances," said an executive.

"Basically, any transaction which we think is suspicious is being reported. We do not want to face the blame," said a bank executive. The government is analysing data on bank deposits and tallying it with tax returns in a bid to go after those who may have sought to use demonetisation as an initiative to deposit unexplained cash.
