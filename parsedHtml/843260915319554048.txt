Legendary Musician Chuck Berry Dead at 90
Chuck Berry, the pioneering rock and roll musician, has died, police confirm. He was 90 years old. In a statement posted to Facebook the Charles County Police Department in Missouri wrote that they responded to a “medical emergency on Buckner Road at approximately 12:40 p.m. today (Saturday, March 18).” That’s where the found Berry, who first responders say was “unresponsive.” Despite administering “lifesaving techniques,” Berry could not be revived. He was pronounced dead at 1:26 p.m. “The St. Charles County Police Department sadly confirms the death of Charles Edward Anderson Berry Sr., better known as legendary musician Chuck Berry,” the statement reads, adding that his family “requests privacy during this time of bereavement.” We’ll have more on this story as it develops.
