7. David Rockefeller, who as the head of Chase Manhattan bank spread the gospel of American capitalism around the world, died at 101.

He was the last surviving grandson of John D. Rockefeller, the founder of the Standard Oil Company and America’s first billionaire.

As an heir to that legacy, Mr. Rockefeller lived in baronial splendor and privilege. As a boy, he and his brothers would roller-skate along Fifth Avenue trailed by a limousine, in case they grew tired.

11. Finally, as if the F.B.I. weren’t busy enough: Tom Brady’s missing Super Bowl jersey was found in Mexico.
