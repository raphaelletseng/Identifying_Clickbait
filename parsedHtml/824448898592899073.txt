The 19-year-old Dunkin' Donuts worker behind Ashley Judd's viral #NastyWoman poem - BBC News


Actress Ashley Judd's performance of the feminist slam poem #NastyWoman was one of the most shared videos of the Women's March in Washington DC.

But alongside the praise, many have condemned the poem - particularly the personal attacks it makes against President Trump.

Trending spoke to the unlikely author of the poem, 19-year-old Dunkin Donuts worker Nina Mariah Donovan from Tennessee.
