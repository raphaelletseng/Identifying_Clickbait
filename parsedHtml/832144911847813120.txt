Air France-KLM Profit Climbs on Fuel-Cost Drop 
Biography Robert Wall @R_Wall robert.wall@wsj.com LONDON—Air France-KLM SA said its profit surged last year on sharply lower fuel costs. Net profit for 2016 was €792 million ($839.8 million), compared with €118 million a year earlier, the Paris-based carrier said Thursday. Its closely watched operating income advanced 34% to €1.05 billion from €780 million. Without currency headwinds, the operating result would have tacked on another €558 million.
